Annual Events in Hyderabad

Hyderabad hosts several cultural, literary, and traditional events that attract locals and tourists alike. Here are some of the most celebrated annual events:

Hyderabad Literature Festival

An annual event that brings together writers, poets, and intellectuals for discussions, book launches, and literary sessions.

Sindh Culture Day

A vibrant celebration of Sindhi heritage featuring Ajrak-clad participants, traditional dances, and folk music.

Urs of Lal Shahbaz Qalandar

A grand religious festival celebrated in Sehwan, near Hyderabad, to honor the Sufi saint with dhamal (spiritual dance), prayers, and music.

Independence Day Celebrations

The city comes alive with flag-hoisting ceremonies, parades, fireworks, and cultural programs on August 14th.
