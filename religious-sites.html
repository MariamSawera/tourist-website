Religious Sites in Hyderabad

Hyderabad is home to several significant religious sites that attract visitors for their spiritual, architectural, and historical importance.

Shah Abdul Latif Bhitai Shrine

Located in Bhit Shah near Hyderabad, this shrine is dedicated to the famous Sindhi Sufi poet, Shah Abdul Latif Bhitai. It is a center for Sufi music and spiritual gatherings.

Makki Shah Mosque

One of the oldest mosques in Hyderabad, known for its stunning architecture and historical significance.

Saint Francis Xavier Cathedral

A beautiful Roman Catholic cathedral that serves as a place of worship and an architectural landmark in the city.

Ramapir Temple

This Hindu temple is a significant place of pilgrimage, attracting devotees from across Sindh, especially during the annual Ramapir festival.

Sadhu Bela Temple

Located on an island in the Indus River, this historic Hindu temple is a serene spot for worship and meditation.

Lal Shahbaz Qalandar Shrine

Located in Sehwan Sharif, not far from Hyderabad, this shrine is dedicated to the famous Sufi saint and is known for its vibrant annual Urs festival.

Jamia Masjid Hyderabad

A grand mosque that serves as a major center for Islamic teachings and Friday prayers.
