Transport in Hyderabad

Hyderabad offers a variety of transport options to help residents and tourists navigate the city efficiently.

Public Buses

The city has an extensive network of public buses operated by the Sindh Transport Department, providing affordable transport across major areas.

Rickshaws

Auto-rickshaws are a common mode of transport for short distances, offering a convenient and economical way to get around.

Taxis

Both traditional yellow taxis and ride-hailing services like Careem and Uber operate in Hyderabad, making commuting easy and comfortable.

Railway Services

The Hyderabad Railway Station connects the city to major destinations across Pakistan, with regular train services available.

Motorbikes and Scooters

Many locals prefer using motorbikes for their daily commute, as they are a quick and cost-effective mode of transportation.
